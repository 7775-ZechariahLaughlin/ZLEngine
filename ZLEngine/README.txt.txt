=== Simple ZLENGINE Game Demonstration ===
Contributors: Zechariah Laughlin (Lead Programmer/Developer)

=== Description ===
This is a simple game made to demonstrate the functions of the ZLENGINE 3D Engine. 
It is a simple walk through rings and collect coins for points adventure.
